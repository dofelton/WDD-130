Overview

Purpose

Our mission is to educate people about white water rafting and then allow them to experience the thrill through our tours. On this site you will be able to research a variety of topics pertaining to rafting and book a trip when you are ready.

Audience

Our audience are people that have heard about white water rafting but want to learn more and experience it for themselves. Why will people visit our site? They will come to read about rafting and to watch videos that show what they will experience. Will they want to purchase a tour? Yes, they will buy tickets that allow them to experience white water rafting. Who are our most targeted customers? Those people that enjoy the outdoors and are looking for a little adventure. They need to be between 12 and 75 years old.
